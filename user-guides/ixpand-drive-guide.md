# iXpand™ Drive Application - User Guide

## Introduction

The perfect companion to any iPhone or iPad, the SanDisk iXpand™ Drive App makes it easy to manage photos, videos, and music directly from various SanDisk iXpand flash drives.

The application also works together with password-protection software to help keep your sensitive files secure across all devices. Take all the photos and videos you want—with the iXpand Drive App you're always ready to take more.

## Supported File Formats

The iXpand Drive App supports the following formats for photos, videos, and music. The music feature combines stored music files on the iXpand Flash Drive along with your iTunes® Library for easy access.

### File Format Support

| Media Type | Supported Formats |
|------------|-------------------|
| **Photos** | BMP, TIF, TIFF, JPG, PNG, GIF, XBM, ICO, TGA |
| **Videos** | WMV, AVI, MKV, MP4, MOV, FLV, MPG, RMVB, M4V, TS |
| **Music** | MP3, AIF, WAV, AIFF, M4A, WMA, AAC, OGG, FLAC |

## Installation

Follow these steps to install the iXpand Drive App on your iOS device:

### Step 1: Connect to the Internet
Ensure your iPhone or iPad is connected to the Internet.

### Step 2: Open the App Store
On your device's home screen, locate and tap the **App Store** icon.

### Step 3: Search for the App
1. At the bottom of the screen, tap **Search**
2. In the search field, type **iXpand Drive**
3. Tap **Search**

### Step 4: Install the App
1. Locate the SanDisk iXpand Drive app icon
2. Tap **Install**
3. Wait for the installation to complete

## Initial Setup Requirements

Initial setup requires a mobile device with a compatible operating system and Internet access.

### Important Notes

⚠️ **iCloud Photo Library:** An iOS device with iCloud Photo Library Sync enabled may take longer than usual during the initial backup process.

💡 **Data Usage:** To avoid additional data charges, upload data over a Wi-Fi® connection.

### Disable Cellular Data Usage

To prevent the iXpand Drive app from using cellular data:

1. On your mobile device, tap the **Settings** icon
2. Tap **Cellular**
3. Locate **iXpand Flash Drive** app
4. Toggle the switch to **Disable**

### Optimize Backup Speed

Backup times can be shortened by temporarily disabling iCloud Sync during the backup process.

**To disable iCloud Sync:**

1. On your mobile device, tap the **Settings** icon
2. Tap **Apple ID, iCloud, iTunes & App Store** menu
3. Tap **iCloud**
4. Tap the **Photos** menu
5. Toggle **iCloud Photo Library** to **Off**

**Remember to re-enable iCloud Photo Library after completing your backup.**

## Using the iXpand™ Drive Application

### App Overview

The iXpand Drive App offers simple navigation through the home screen, allowing quick access to main tasks and features.

#### Key Features

- **📷 Camera Integration:** Tap the Camera icon to take photos/videos and directly save them onto the iXpand Flash Drive
- **⚙️ Settings:** Tap the App Settings icon for additional help or to customize settings
- **🔄 Action Cards:** Quick shortcuts to frequently used features like Back Up & Restore
- **📋 Home Screen Menu:** Lists all available actions and operations

#### Core Functionality

- Copy Files
- View Files
- Back Up and Restore Files

### Opening the iXpand Drive App

To launch the iXpand Drive App:

1. Tap the **SanDisk iXpand Drive App** icon on your home screen
2. Accept the **SanDisk License Agreement**

**Note:** The License Agreement only appears when first using the app and must be accepted before proceeding.

## Best Practices

### Data Management
- Regularly back up important photos and videos
- Organize files into folders for easy access
- Use the app's automatic backup feature for convenience

### Storage Optimization
- Delete unnecessary files to free up space
- Transfer large video files to the iXpand Drive to preserve device storage
- Monitor available storage on both your device and the iXpand Drive

### Security
- Enable password protection for sensitive files
- Keep the iXpand Drive App updated to the latest version
- Safely eject the iXpand Flash Drive before disconnecting

## Troubleshooting

### App Won't Install
- Verify your device meets minimum system requirements
- Ensure you have sufficient storage space on your device
- Check your internet connection

### Slow Backup Performance
- Disable iCloud Photo Library during backup (see Initial Setup section)
- Use Wi-Fi instead of cellular data
- Close other apps running in the background

### Files Not Appearing
- Verify file format is supported (see Supported File Formats)
- Ensure the iXpand Flash Drive is properly connected
- Restart the app and try again
